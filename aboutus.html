ABOUT US

The Meyyarasu College of Arts & Science was established by the benevolent philanthropist Thiru K. Ram, the Chairman of National Educational and Charitable Trust, Madurai. This trust was established in 1984-1985 under the Chairmanship of Thiru K. Ram. The first educational institution originated from the trust was National Engineering College. The Chairman decided to start a self-financing Arts & Science College in the style and name of Meyyarasu College of Arts & Science (Co-Education). The College is being constructed in the land of 10 acres and 5.5 cents in Madurai on the National High ways NH7 at a distance of about 4KM from Madurai town, amidst a good surroundings in the rural area. The aim of this institution is to impart higher education to economically poor students in the vicinity of Madurai.
