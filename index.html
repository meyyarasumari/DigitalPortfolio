welcome

The Meyyarasu College of Arts & Science was established by the benevolent philanthropist Thiru K. Ram, the Chairman of National Educational and Charitable Trust, Madurai. This trust was established in 1984-1985 under the Chairmanship of Thiru K. Ram. The first educational institution originated from the trust was National Engineering College. The Chairman decided to start a self-financing Arts & Science College in the style and name of Meyyarasu College of Arts & Science (Co-Education). The College is being constructed in the land of 10 acres and 5.5 cents in Madurai on the National High ways NH7 at a distance of about 4KM from Madurai town, amidst a good surroundings in the rural area. The aim of this institution is to impart higher education to economically poor students in the vicinity of Madurai. The Government of Tamil Nadu has granted permission to start this Co-Educational institution in the year 1997-1998 vide its Order No. MS. 315 dated 13.06.1997. The Manonmaniam Sundaranar University, Tirunelveli has granted affiliation to this college vide its Letter No. MSU/ CD/ Fresh-Aff/ 97-98 dated 22-08-1997. The College started functioning from 29-08-1997. Now, our college has completed 2 decades of academic service to rural students.
